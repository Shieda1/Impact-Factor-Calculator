What is the impact factor calculator?
The impact factor calculator is a tool that calculates the importance of a journal.
How to calculate impact factor?
To calculate impact factor you should use the following formula:
impact factor = total citations in nth year / (citable publications in n1 year + citable publications in n2 year)
Why use impact factor calculator?
The impact factor calculator is used in order to determine the importance of a journal.
